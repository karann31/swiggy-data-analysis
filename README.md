# 🍽️ Swiggy Sales Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Swiggy sales data to understand customer behavior, food preferences, delivery trends, and revenue performance across different locations and time periods.

The goal is to generate actionable insights using KPIs and visual analytics to improve business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To analyze Swiggy sales performance across multiple dimensions such as time, geography, food type, and customer behavior in order to identify trends, patterns, and optimization opportunities.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- **Total Sales** – Overall revenue generated from all orders  
- **Average Sales** – Average revenue per order  
- **Total Orders** – Total number of orders placed  
- **Average Ratings** – Customer satisfaction score  

---

## 📈 Charts & Analysis

### 1. Monthly Sales Trend
**Objective:** Shows how total sales fluctuate month by month.  
👉 Helps identify seasonal demand patterns and growth trends.

---

### 2. Daily Sales Trend
**Objective:** Highlights order and revenue variations across days of the week.  
👉 Helps identify peak and low-demand days.

---

### 3. Total Sales by Food Type (Veg vs Non-Veg)
**Objective:** Compare revenue contribution by cuisine type.  
👉 Understand customer food preference behavior.

---

### 4. Total Sales by State (Map Visualization)
**Objective:** Display state-wise revenue distribution.  
👉 Helps identify high-performing regions geographically.

---

### 5. Quarterly Performance Summary
**Objective:** Combine sales, ratings, and orders by quarter.  
👉 Evaluates overall business performance over time.

---

### 6. Top 5 Cities by Sales
**Objective:** Identify leading cities contributing the most revenue.  
👉 Highlights key business markets.

---

### 7. Weekly Trend Analysis
**Objective:** Monitor weekly fluctuations in sales to identify consistency or peak periods.  
👉 Helps understand demand stability.

---
